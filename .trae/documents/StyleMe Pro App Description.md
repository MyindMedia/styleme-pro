This app is **FitCheck**, a comprehensive AI-powered personal stylist and digital wardrobe manager built with **React Native (Expo)**, **TypeScript**, and **Supabase**.

**Core Purpose:**
To help users digitize their closet, organize their wardrobe, and receive personalized AI outfit recommendations based on their clothes, mood, and local weather.

**Key Features & Functions:**

1. **Digital Closet Management:**

   * Upload and categorize clothing items (Tops, Bottoms, Shoes, etc.).

   * AI-powered background removal for clean item images.

   * Detailed item tracking (Brand, Price, Cost-per-wear, Seasons).

2. **AI Styling & Outfit Generation:**

   * **"Style Me":** Generates outfit suggestions based on user-selected "Moods" (e.g., Old Money, Streetwear, Corporate) using the user's own items.

   * **Weather Look:** Recommends outfits based on real-time local weather conditions.

   * **Outfit Shuffle:** Randomly generates outfit combinations to rediscover forgotten clothes.

3. **Virtual Try-On & Tools:**

   * **Virtual Try-On:** Users can visualize items on themselves (Selfie Capture/Gallery).

   * **Packing List:** Helps users plan and pack outfits for trips.

   * **Wishlist:** Save and track desired items.

   * **Analytics:** Insights into wardrobe value, most worn items, and category breakdown.

4. **Community & Social:**

   * Share outfits and get inspiration from other users.

5. **Monetization (RevenueCat):**

   * **Freemium Model:** Basic features are free.

   * **Pro Subscription:** Unlocks unlimited storage, advanced AI styling, background removal, and detailed analytics.

**Tech Stack:**

* **Frontend:** React Native, Expo Router, NativeWind (Tailwind CSS).

* **Backend:** Supabase (Auth, Database, Storage), tRPC, Express.

* **AI/Services:** Firecrawl (Web scraping), RevenueCat (In-App Purchases), Expo Camera/Location.
