# Indyx App Research

## Overview
Indyx is a digital wardrobe and styling app that focuses on cataloging, styling, and reselling clothes. It positions itself as a "one-stop wardrobe app" with both free and premium tiers.

## Core Features

### Free Tier Features
- **Wardrobe Cataloging**: Upload unlimited items with automatic background removal
- **Outfit Building**: Create and save outfit combinations manually
- **Calendar/Outfit Logging**: Track what you wear each day
- **Resale Marketplace**: Buy and sell secondhand items peer-to-peer
- **Collections**: Organize items into custom collections

### Premium "Insider" Features ($60/year)
- **Analytics Dashboard**: In-depth wardrobe statistics
  - Total closet value
  - Cost-per-wear calculations
  - Most worn items
  - Closet composition breakdown
  - Usage patterns
- **Outfit Selfies**: Save photos of yourself in outfits
- **Inspiration Boards**: Save outfit inspiration
- **Wishlist Integration**: Visualize potential purchases with current wardrobe

### Professional Services
- **Pro-Indyx Cataloging**: $195 for up to 100 items (professional comes to your home)
- **Personal Stylist Lookbooks**: Human stylists create custom outfit suggestions
- Available in SF Bay Area, NYC, Los Angeles

## What Users Love (from reviews)

1. **Automatic Background Removal**: "Lightyears ahead of Stylebook" - clean, professional-looking photos
2. **Clean UI/UX**: Intuitive, modern interface
3. **Calendar Tracking**: Reveals which pieces "earn their keep"
4. **Cost-Per-Wear Analytics**: Helps justify purchases and identify underused items
5. **Human Stylist Option**: Real stylists create personalized lookbooks
6. **Unlimited Items**: No caps on free tier for items/outfits
7. **Community Features**: Can view other users' closets for inspiration

## What Users Complain About / Want

1. **No Outfit Randomizer**: Users want a "swipe to mix and match" feature like Whering has
2. **No AI Outfit Suggestions**: "It gives no outfit suggestions, you still have to manually create outfits"
3. **Analytics Behind Paywall**: Basic stats should be free
4. **No Automatic Outfit Generation**: Must manually create all outfits
5. **Limited Sorting/Filtering**: Want better organization options
6. **No Weather Integration**: Want outfit suggestions based on weather
7. **No Packing List Feature**: Mentioned as missing compared to some competitors
8. **Slow Background Removal**: Sometimes takes time to process

## Competitor Comparison Insights

### Indyx vs Stylebook
- Indyx: Better background removal, newer/evolving, community features
- Stylebook: Better outfit scheduling, more established

### Indyx vs Whering
- Indyx: Cleaner UI, human stylists available
- Whering: Has randomizer feature, AI suggestions

### Indyx vs Acloset
- Indyx: More analytics, resale marketplace
- Acloset: AI-powered outfit suggestions
